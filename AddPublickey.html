Guide to add gpg key

Please make sure you have a terminal like Newterm installed on your device and that you have wget or wget2 installed.

Open your terminal on your device and type su to enter root the defualt password is alpine but I recommend you change it.

type cd Documents then type wget https://framdark.github.io/frams/Framdark.pub or if you have wget2 installed then you would do wget2 instead of wget.

Then type apt-key add Framdark.pub then apt-get update and your done now your free to add the repo and enjoy a secure expirence.
